UP Day — Three Wonders
▼
3h 30m drivingSleep: St. IgnaceStart 7:30 AM — fills by 9 AM!
🌊
Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore
7:30 AM · 2–5 hrs
MILD
Miners Castle overlook (5-min walk) + Miners Beach. Boat tour — 2.5 hrs seated, best view of the cliffs.
MODERATE
Miners Castle + Miners Beach + Munising Falls (~2 hrs). Afternoon boat tour if pre-booked.
HIGH
Chapel Loop — 10 miles: Chapel Falls, Chapel Rock arch, Lake Superior shoreline. Start 7 AM. Best UP hike.
💎
Kitch-iti-kipi — The Big Spring
~11:00 AM · 45 min–1.5 hrs
MILD
Raft ride over 40-ft emerald spring. 10,000 gal/min bubbling through limestone. The "Mirror of Heaven."
MODERATE
Raft ride + 0.3-mi boardwalk loop + Indian Lake State Park beach (5 min away).
HIGH
Raft ride + Indian Lake kayak rental (100 yards away). Paddle the south bay — 1.5–2 hrs on the water.
⏰ Arrive before 9 AM or after 5 PM — midday waits run 1–2 hrs on holidays. M-149, 12 mi west of Manistique.
💦
Tahquamenon Falls State Park
~1:30 PM · 1.5–4 hrs
MILD
Upper Falls platform — 5-min walk, 200-ft wide amber waterfall. 2nd-largest east of Mississippi.
MODERATE
Upper Falls + rowboat at Lower Falls — eye-level with the cascade from the water. ~2 hrs total.
HIGH
Full 9-mile North Country Trail loop, Upper + Lower Falls via the ridge. Trekking poles recommended.
🌉
St. Ignace Evening
~5:00 PM
MILD
Dinner at Bentley's Tavern with Straits views. Watch bridge lights come on from the waterfront park.
MODERATE
Castle Rock overlook (100 steps, panoramic bridge + island view) before dinner.
HIGH
Evening trail run on North Country Trail west of town — pine ridgeline, almost always deserted at dusk.
🌙

Mackinac Island
All day · 5–8 hrs
MILD
Ferry from St. Ignace, horse carriage tour ($35/pp), Grand Hotel porch lunch, fudge on Main Street. No cars anywhere.
MODERATE
Rent bikes, flat 8-mile perimeter road with Lake Huron views, hike Fort Mackinac, lunch at the Carriage House.
HIGH
Perimeter + interior trails (Tranquil Bluff, Crack-in-the-Island, Soldier's Garden) — 18–22 mi total.
⛴ Book ferry at sheplersferry.com. Take evening ferry back (~6–8 PM) then cross bridge south.
💎

MILD

MODERATE
Gaslight District walk + beach (hunt for Petoskey stones — fossilized coral, unique to this area).
HIGH
Sunset jog on Little Traverse Wheelway through Bay Harbor — paved, lake views, 4 mi out/back.

Sleeping Bear Dunes → Holland
▼
2h 45m drivingSleep: Holland/SaugatuckDunes by 8:30 AM — fills by 10!
🏔
Pierce Stocking Scenic Drive
8:30 AM · 1–1.5 hrs
MILD
Drive the 7.4-mi loop. Stop 9 (Lake Michigan Overlook) is the best view in Michigan — 5 min on foot.
MODERATE
Walk the ridge trail at Stop 9 (30 min) + Stop 4 (Glen Lakes overlook). Both stunning.
HIGH
Park at Stop 9, descend dune to Lake Michigan and climb back — 650 ft of sand. Budget 1.5 hrs.
🏜
Sleeping Bear Dune Climb
45 min–2.5 hrs
MILD
Walk to the base. The scale in person stops everyone cold — nothing prepares you for it.
MODERATE
Climb to first ridge (~130 ft). Glen Lakes panorama behind you. Worth every step. 45 min.
HIGH
Run to Lake Michigan and back — 4-mile, 450+ ft of sand. Hardest easy hike in Michigan. Bring 2L water.
🍔
Glen Arbor — Art's Tavern
~12:30 PM · 1–3 hrs
MILD
Legendary burgers since 1934. Drive around Little Glen Lake for the postcard view. Cash only!
MODERATE
Kayak Crystal River (2 mi, very gentle, ends at Lake Michigan). Rentals at Crystal River Outfitters.
HIGH
Kayak Crystal River to the lake, then bike Sleeping Bear Heritage Trail back (~15-mi loop).
🌅
Saugatuck & Holland
~4:00 PM · 2–3 hrs
MILD
$1 ferry across Kalamazoo River, wine at Singapore Bank Winery, sunset dinner at Coral Gables.
MODERATE
Climb Mount Baldhead (300 steps, golden-hour Lake Michigan views), then Tunnel Park beach at dusk.
HIGH
Rent bikes in Holland, ride Laketown Trail to the lakeshore and back (~14 mi), late dinner in Douglas.

Every traffic pattern works in your favor
The farthest-first routing turns all Memorial Day congestion into advantages. Here's the breakdown day by day.

Friday northbound · 2–8 PM
Your biggest single advantage of the trip
Heading north on a holiday Friday afternoon is counterintuitive — and that's why it works. All outbound traffic flows south toward Detroit, Chicago, and the beaches. US-131 and I-75 northbound will be light. You arrive at Pictured Rocks hours before Saturday's crowd.
