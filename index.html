Grand Island — Ferry + Hike
8:00 AM · Half-day
MILD
Take the ferry over and ride a rental bike along the shoreline trail to view the 300-ft sandstone cliffs and East Channel Lighthouse.
MODERATE
Ferry + North Country Trail hike to the cliff overlooks. Stunning elevated views of Lake Superior and the Pictured Rocks coast.
HIGH
Full island loop hike (~8 mi) covering North Beach, the cliffs, East Channel Lighthouse and the interior ridge. Bring water + snacks.
🔗 View Info · grandislandup.com
Ferry departs from Munising dock · ~$25/pp round-trip · arrives Munising side of Pictured Rocks.
💦
Munising Falls + Wagner Falls
Late AM · ~1 hr total
MILD
Munising Falls: 5-min paved walk to a stunning 50-ft curtain drop framed in sandstone. Then Wagner Falls Scenic Site (off M-94, 2 min).
MODERATE
Both falls + the full 0.5-mi Munising Falls canyon loop. Arrive early — they're magnificent with no crowd.
HIGH
Both falls back-to-back then add Sand Point Beach jog before heading to Miners Castle.
🔗 View Info · Munising Falls NPS
🌊
Miners Castle Overlook
~11:00 AM · 1–2 hrs
MILD
The best land-based view of the Pictured Rocks cliffs. 5-min paved walk — completely accessible, completely jaw-dropping.
MODERATE
Miners Castle + Miners Beach walk + pre-booked Pictured Rocks boat tour (2.5 hrs, best from-water view).
HIGH
Chapel Loop — 10 miles: Chapel Falls, Chapel Rock arch, Lake Superior shoreline. The best single UP hike.
🔗 View Info · Miners Castle NPS
💎
Kitch-iti-kipi — The Big Spring
~1:30 PM · 1 hr
MILD
Self-propelled observation raft over 40-ft emerald spring. 10,000 gal/min bubbling through limestone — the "Mirror of Heaven."
MODERATE
Raft ride + 0.3-mi boardwalk loop + Indian Lake State Park beach nearby.
HIGH
Raft ride + Indian Lake kayak rental (100 yards away). Paddle the south bay — 1.5–2 hrs on the water.
🔗 View Info · Palms Book State Park
📍 M-149, 12 mi west of Manistique · $17 Recreation Passport · Arrive before 9 AM or after 5 PM to avoid waits.
🚶
Huron Boardwalk · St. Ignace
~5:30 PM · 30 min
MILD
Stroll the Huron Boardwalk along the waterfront. Mackinac Bridge glows at dusk — perfect end to an epic UP day.
MODERATE
Boardwalk + Castle Rock overlook (100 steps, panoramic bridge + island view) then dinner at Bentley's Tavern.
HIGH
Boardwalk + North Country Trail evening run west of town — pine ridgeline, deserted at dusk.

Straits State Park — Bridge Views
8:00 AM · 30 min
MILD
Morning stop at Straits State Park for the best panoramic view of the Mackinac Bridge from land. Free with Recreation Passport.
MODERATE
Walk the bluff trail for multiple bridge angles. The early morning light on the bridge before crowds is magical.
HIGH
Morning run through Straits State Park trail network — best ridge views before the island ferry.
🔗 View Info · Straits State Park
🏝
Mackinac Island — Full Day
9:30 AM–6 PM
MILD
Ferry from St. Ignace dock, horse carriage tour ($35/pp), Grand Hotel porch lunch, fudge on Main Street. No cars — pure peace.
MODERATE
Rent bikes, flat 8-mile perimeter road with Lake Huron views, hike Fort Mackinac, lunch at the Carriage House.
HIGH
Perimeter road + interior trails (Tranquil Bluff, Crack-in-the-Island, Soldier's Garden) — 18–22 mi total. Last ferry ~8 PM.
🔗 Book Ferry · sheplersferry.com
Take evening ferry back (~6–8 PM) then cross bridge south — Sunday evening is moderate but moving.
🌲
M-119 Tunnel of Trees + Legs Inn
Evening drive
MILD
Drive the magical M-119 from Cross Village south — a canopy of trees arching over the road with Lake Michigan glimpses. Michigan's most beautiful road.
MODERATE
Stop at Legs Inn in Cross Village for a drink on the terrace. Folk-art exterior + stunning lake views. Then continue the Tunnel of Trees south.
HIGH
Legs Inn stop + pull off for a short bluff trail hike along M-119 with Lake Michigan views. Hunt Petoskey stones on the beach below.
🔗 View Info · Legs Inn 🔗 Tunnel of Trees
🏘
Petoskey — Gaslight District
Evening · 30 min
MILD
Stroll the charming Victorian Gaslight District. Best small-town energy in northern Michigan.
MODERATE
Gaslight District + Petoskey State Park beach. Hunt for Petoskey stones — fossilized coral unique to this shore.
HIGH
Jog the Little Traverse Wheelway rail trail through Bay Harbor — paved, lake views, 4 mi out/back.
🔗 View Info · Downtown Petoskey

Empire Bluff Trail
8:30 AM · 1.5-mi round-trip · ~1 hr
MILD
0.75-mi hike to a sweeping Lake Michigan panorama from a 400-ft bluff. Maximum payoff for minimal effort — the hidden gem of Sleeping Bear.
MODERATE
Full 1.5-mi out-and-back. Linger at the top — Lake Michigan + dune panorama at sunrise is extraordinary.
HIGH
Run Empire Bluff trail then jog down to Empire Beach before driving to Pierce Stocking. Start 8 AM for best light.
🔗 View Info · Empire Bluff NPS
🚗
Pierce Stocking Scenic Drive
10:00 AM · Overlooks #9 & #10
MILD
Drive the 7.4-mi loop. Overlook #9 (Lake Michigan Overlook, 450-ft bluff) is the single best view in Michigan — 5 min on foot from parking.
MODERATE
Stop at both Overlooks #9 and #10 (Dune Overlook with Glen Lake view). Walk the ridge trails at each. ~1 hr total.
HIGH
Park at Overlook #9, descend the dune face to Lake Michigan and climb back — 650 ft, brutal and legendary. Budget 1.5 hrs.
🔗 View Info · Pierce Stocking NPS
🏜
Sleeping Bear Dune Climb
11:30 AM · 45 min–2.5 hrs
MILD
Walk to the base. The scale in person stops everyone — nothing prepares you for it.
MODERATE
Climb to the first ridge (~130 ft). Glen Lake panorama behind you. Worth every step. 45 min.
HIGH
Run to Lake Michigan and back — 4-mile, 450+ ft of sand. Hardest easy hike in Michigan. Bring 2L water.
🔗 View Info · Sleeping Bear NPS
🔭
Arcadia Overlook — Inspiration Point
~2:30 PM · 15-min pull-off
MILD
Pull off on US-31 south near Arcadia for a stunning elevated Lake Michigan bluff view. One of the best roadside stops in Michigan.
MODERATE
Short walk from the overlook parking to the bluff edge. Views stretch 30+ miles on a clear day.
HIGH
Overlook + short trail scramble down to the beach below. Then continue south to Holland.

Windmill Island Gardens — De Zwaan
9:00 AM · Opens 9 AM
MILD
See the authentic 250-year-old Dutch windmill "De Zwaan" — the only working Dutch windmill in the US. Beautiful tulip gardens surrounding it.
MODERATE
Full Windmill Island loop + windmill tour. Interactive demonstrations of the milling process. ~1.5 hrs.
HIGH
Windmill Island + 8th Street shops for Dutch souvenirs + breakfast at a local bakery before the lighthouse.
🔗 View Info · windmillisland.org
🏮
Holland State Park — Big Red Lighthouse
11:00 AM · Final Lake Michigan views
MILD
Walk to the Big Red Lighthouse — Holland's iconic red beacon on Lake Michigan. Perfect last photo of the trip. Sit on the beach and breathe it all in.
MODERATE
Big Red + Tunnel Park beach walk (the dune tunnel is unique — you walk through a dune to reach the lake).
HIGH
Morning run along Holland State Park beach trail, lighthouse loop, then brunch on 8th Street before the airport.
🔗 View Info · Holland State Park
